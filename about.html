👷 Our Story

Built for the Trades.
Run by Someone Who Gets It.

Nexora exists because home service contractors deserve the same growth infrastructure that big companies use — without needing a marketing department to run it.

👤
The Founder

Rafael Guerrero

Based in Green Bay, WI — Nexora was built out of a real frustration. Watching hard-working home service contractors spend thousands on ads with no system to capture and close the leads that came in. Watching them miss calls at 7pm because there was no one to answer. Watching them follow up once and never again because life got in the way.

The tools to fix this existed. Big enterprise companies were using AI, automated CRMs, and sophisticated ad funnels to dominate their markets. But for the HVAC contractor with a crew of four, or the roofing company doing $1.2M a year? That infrastructure felt out of reach — too expensive, too complex, too much to manage.

Nexora changes that. We take the same systems used by growth-obsessed companies and make them accessible, manageable, and built specifically for the trades. You don't need a marketing degree. You don't need to learn new software. You just need to show up for the jobs we put in your calendar.

"Every contractor deserves a system that works as hard as they do."

Our Mission

Level the playing field
for home service businesses.

The biggest contractors in your market aren't winning because they're better at the work. They're winning because they have better systems. We're here to fix that.

For the Contractor Who Outworks Everyone

You're great at what you do. You show up early, do quality work, and have happy customers. What you shouldn't have to be great at is marketing technology. That's our job — we handle the systems so you can focus on the work.

📈

Real Growth, Not Vanity Metrics

We don't report on impressions or reach. We report on leads, bookings, and revenue. If the system isn't putting money in your pocket, it's not working — and we won't pretend otherwise. Results or we fix it.
